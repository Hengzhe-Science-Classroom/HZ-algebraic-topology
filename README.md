# Algebraic Topology · Interactive

**Homotopy, homology, and cohomology**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 5 · Research Frontier

## What You'll Learn

- The fundamental group and van Kampen's theorem
- Covering spaces
- Simplicial and singular homology
- Cohomology and cup product
- Poincaré duality
- Higher homotopy groups (intro)

## References

- Allen Hatcher, *Algebraic Topology* (Cambridge, free online)
- William S. Massey, *A Basic Course in Algebraic Topology* (GTM 127, Springer)
- James W. Vick, *Homology Theory* (GTM 145, Springer)

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos
- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
